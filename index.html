About INSIGHT

The 1st International Conference on Intelligent Systems, Signal Processing, and Smart Grid Technologies (INSIGHT 2025) is an academic and industry-focused event that brings together researchers, engineers, innovators, and experts from around the world to discuss the latest advancements in intelligent systems, signal processing, and smart grid technologies. The conference aims to provide a dynamic platform for knowledge exchange, fostering collaborations that address both current and future challenges in these transformative fields. In today’s rapidly evolving technological landscape, intelligent systems and smart grid technologies are revolutionizing industries, shaping the future of energy, communications, and automation. This conference not only serves as an academic event but also as a catalyst for industry-academia collaborations aimed at solving global challenges, enhancing energy efficiency, and advancing smart technologies that contribute to a sustainable future.

About Vardhaman

Vardhaman College of Engineering (VCE) was established in the year 1999 by Vardhaman Educational Society, Hyderabad. It is a UGC Autonomous college, approved by AICTE, and is permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad. We offer undergraduate B.Tech programmes in CSE, CSE (AI&ML), AI&ML, AI&DS, IT, ECE, EEE, ME and CE of total intake of 1380; postgraduate M.Tech programmes with specialisation in DECS, PEED, CSE, SE, and ED; MBA programme of total intake of 150; and doctoral programmes in CSE, ECE and ME. The college is accredited by NAAC at A++ grade with a CGPA of 3.58 on a scale of 4. Six of its UG engineering programmes – B. Tech ECE, EEE, CSE, ME, CE, and IT are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi under the Tier-1 category. VCE is the most sought-after institute to make the dreams fulfilled for many aspiring engineers. Our major strength lies in imparting quality education to the global standards and envisages to address various societal needs. Visit www.vardhaman.org
